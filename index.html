Why do we have to see doctor?

To diagnose and treat illnesses: Doctors are trained to diagnose and treat a wide range of illnesses and medical conditions. They use their expertise and knowledge to identify the underlying causes of symptoms and provide appropriate treatment options to help their patients recover.

To perform medical procedures: Doctors are also trained to perform medical procedures, such as surgeries, biopsies, and diagnostic tests. They have the necessary skills and experience to perform these procedures safely and effectively.
